Polynomial achieves lower training MSE because sin(2πx) is nonlinear — linear regression suffers from high bias.

Key insight: A degree-1 (linear) model underfits a sinusoidal curve — it has high bias. A polynomial model of sufficient degree captures the curvature and achieves much lower training MSE. The correct answer is Polynomial regression.
