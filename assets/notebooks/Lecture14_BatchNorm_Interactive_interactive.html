Batch Normalization — Section 8.3.0.1

Batch Normalization normalizes activations within a mini-batch, then applies a learnable scale (γ) and shift (β). Adjust the parameters to see how the distribution changes.

Step 1 — Mini-batch statistics

μ_B = (1/m) Σ x_i
σ²_B = (1/m) Σ (x_i − μ_B)²

Step 2 — Normalize

x̂_i = (x_i − μ_B) / √(σ²_B + ε)

Step 3 — Scale and shift

y_i = γ · x̂_i + β
